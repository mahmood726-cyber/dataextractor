RCTExtractor v4.9.1-AI Validation Study

Comparing extraction accuracy against ground truth from published RCTs

Validation Methodology

Ground Truth Source: Manually verified values from 5 landmark cardiovascular trials published in NEJM.

Fields Validated:

  • Effect Measure (HR value)
  • 95% CI Lower Bound
  • 95% CI Upper Bound
  • Treatment Group N
  • Control Group N
  • Mean Age
  • Male Percentage
  • Trial Registration Number

Accuracy Calculation: Field-level accuracy with tolerances (numeric: 1-2%, counts: ±5)

Comparison Benchmarks:

  • RobotReviewer: 71% overall accuracy (Marshall et al., JAMIA 2016)
  • ChatGPT-4o: 89-91% agreement (PLOS One 2025)
  • Human dual extraction: 78.3% inter-rater agreement
